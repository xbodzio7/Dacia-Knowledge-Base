\# DKB Data Quality Checklist



Before each commit:



\- \[ ] CSV parses correctly

\- \[ ] IDs are unique

\- \[ ] Keys are unique

\- \[ ] Category exists in domains.csv

\- \[ ] Type exists in value\_types.csv

\- \[ ] Unit exists in units.csv

\- \[ ] Descriptions are complete

\- \[ ] Conventional Commit used
