## Browser verification

The exact HTML extracted from the release archive was exercised in Chromium. The initial catalogue contained 67 configurations and 107 visible equipment choices. Selecting Jogger produced 22 configurations and reduced the visible equipment facet to 53 source-backed choices. Selecting one compatible equipment item retained eight results. Three visible configurations were selected and compared in one table with three configuration columns. Model illustrations were present in the selector, result cards and comparison. No JavaScript console errors occurred.

The inline SVG illustrations are deterministic model identifiers generated by repository code. They are not official vehicle photographs and do not represent a specific paint colour or trim.
