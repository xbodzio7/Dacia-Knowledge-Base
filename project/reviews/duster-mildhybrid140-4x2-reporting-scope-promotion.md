# Duster Mild Hybrid 140 4x2 Reporting Scope Promotion

## Selection

The final portfolio review selected the current mild hybrid 140 4x2 manual group
as the seventh explicit Duster reporting subset. Expression, extreme and journey
share one source-backed technical denominator and provide three comparison pairs
without mixing powertrains.

## Explicit scope

`data/reporting/duster_mildhybrid140_4x2_completeness.json` selects exactly three
active configurations and one registered source. Its denominator contains 14
technical attribute/context slots, 58 equipment attributes and one dated gross
catalogue price per configuration.

The source does not state standing-kilometre performance for this group. That
field is excluded from the denominator rather than treated as a missing
observation. The companion evidence specification contains no decisions because
the promoted scope has no technical or equipment gaps.

## Artifact contract

Quality publishes separate completeness, source-coverage and comparison JSON
and Markdown files plus a flat comparison-differences CSV under the
`duster-mildhybrid140-4x2` prefix. All seven files are included in the existing
Quality artifact manifest.

Every earlier Sandero and Duster artifact namespace and denominator remains
unchanged.

## Portfolio completion

All seven homogeneous Duster powertrain groups and all 24 source-supported
Duster configurations now belong to an explicit independent reporting
portfolio. The portfolios remain separate because the source-stated technical
denominators range from 14 to 21 slots and contain different fuel and hybrid
component contexts.

Cross-powertrain aggregation is not introduced by this package. Any consolidated
Duster reporting contract requires a separate future review of common versus
powertrain-specific semantics.
